=== SLIDE 3 ===
THE PROBLEM — REAL WORLD CONTEXT
This isn't just our observation.
These are documented facts — not assumptions we made up for the hackathon.
NITI Aayog · 2024
It's a National Priority
NITI Aayog's 2024 report officially flagged the scheme-discovery gap as a national problem that needs fixing urgently. This is the government itself saying the current system isn't working.
MyScheme.gov.in
The Portal Exists. It Just Has Zero Intelligence.
India already has an official scheme portal — but it's literally just a search box. No eligibility matching, no document guidance, no language support, no conflict detection. Nothing smart.
Union Budget 2025
Money Has Been Allocated
The Union Budget 2025 specifically allocated funds for AI-powered citizen services. There's a real, documented path from what we're building here to actual government adoption.

=== SLIDE 4 ===
OBJECTIVE
What We're Trying to Solve
Build a system where any citizen — regardless of education level, language, or tech literacy — can describe their situation in plain words and instantly know which government schemes they qualify for, what documents they need, and how to actually apply.
Specifically, we want to:
01
Match citizen profiles to eligible schemes automatically — with reasoning
02
Generate a step-by-step application checklist for each matched scheme
03
Auto-fill draft application forms using uploaded identity documents
04
Detect and explicitly flag conflicts between Central and State scheme versions

=== SLIDE 11 ===
OUR SOLUTION — KEY FEATURES
What PolicyPilot Actually Does
01
Plain Language Input
You say it in your own words — or speak it. No dropdowns, no forms. The agent figures out the rest from what you've said.
02
Real PDF Grounding
Every recommendation traces back to an exact clause in an official government PDF. No guessing, no paraphrasing from memory.
03
Conflict Detection
When Central and State versions of the same scheme contradict each other, we flag it explicitly — never silently pick one over the other.
04
Auto-filled Draft Form
Upload your Aadhaar and income certificate. We extract your details and pre-fill the application form. You just review and submit.
05
Step-by-step Checklist
Not just 'you qualify' — we tell you exactly which documents to bring, which office to visit, and what the deadline is for each scheme.
06
Hindi & Regional Languages
Plain-language summary of all results in Hindi or your preferred regional language — no legal jargon, no English barrier.
