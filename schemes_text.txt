Education schemes / official links
1. National Scholarship Portal (main portal)
This is the main official portal where many education scholarships are listed and applied for.
Link: https://scholarships.gov.in
2. Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship for College and University Students
Official Ministry of Education scholarship for meritorious students from poor families.
Link: www.education.gov.in 
3. National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship Scheme (NMMSS)
Official school-level merit scholarship page from Department of School Education.
Link: https://dsel.education.gov.in
4. AICTE Pragati Scholarship Scheme for Girl Students (Technical Degree/Diploma)
Listed officially on NSP; supports girl students in technical education. 
Link: https://scholarships.gov.in
5.  AICTE Saksham Scholarship Scheme for Specially Abled Students (Technical Degree/Diploma)
Official AICTE scholarship on NSP for specially abled students. 
Link: https://scholarships.gov.in
6. AICTE Swanath Scholarship Scheme (Technical Degree/Diploma)
Official NSP-listed AICTE scholarship. 
Link: https://scholarships.gov.in
7.PM-USP Special Scholarship Scheme for Jammu, Kashmir and Ladakh
Officially listed on NSP. 
Link: https://scholarships.gov.in
8. National Scholarship for Post Graduate Studies
Official UGC scholarship listed on NSP. 
Link: https://scholarships.gov.in
9.  Ishan Uday Special Scholarship Scheme for NER
Official scholarship for North Eastern Region students, listed on NSP. 
Link: https://scholarships.gov.in
10. Top Class Education Scheme for SC Students
Official guideline and NSP-linked scholarship for SC students.
Link: https://scholarships.gov.in
